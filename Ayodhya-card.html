Ayodhya Temple

Previous Next

The Ayodhya Temple, also known as the Ram Mandir, is a grand Hindu temple located in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh. It is dedicated to Lord Rama, who is believed to have been born at this sacred site. The temple stands on the Ram Janmabhoomi, considered the exact birthplace of Lord Rama. Its construction began in August 2020 and is designed in the traditional Nagara style using pink sandstone. The temple has become a powerful symbol of faith, unity, and devotion for Hindus across the world. With its spiritual significance and architectural beauty, the Ayodhya Temple has emerged as a major pilgrimage and tourist destination in India.
