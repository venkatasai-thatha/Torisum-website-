Favourite Temples

Tirupati Temple

Tirupati Temple, located in Andhra Pradesh, it is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara.It is one of the richest and most visited temples in the world.

Ayodhya Temple

Ayodhya Ram Mandir is a grand temple dedicated to Lord Ram, located in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, the believed birthplace of Lord Ram.

Dwaraka Temple

Dwarkadhish Temple, is a sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, located in Dwarka, Gujarat.

Kedarnath Temple

Kedarnath Temple is a sacred shrine of Lord Shiva, located in the Himalayas of Uttarakhand.
