TIRUPATI TEMPLE

Previous Next

Tirupati Temple, also known as Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple, is located in Tirumala, Andhra Pradesh. It is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The temple is one of the richest and most visited religious sites in the world. It is built in Dravidian style and situated on the Seshachalam Hills. Devotees often offer their hair as a form of devotion. The famous laddu prasadam is a special attraction. The temple is managed by Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD).
